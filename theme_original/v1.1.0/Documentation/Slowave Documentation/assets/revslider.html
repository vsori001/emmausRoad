Revolution Slider & The Page Builder

Using revolution slider with the page builder is incredibly easy. First, make yourself a slider, or import my demo slider.

Next, fire up the page builder on a page, add a row, and then, look through the "all items" list until you see the "revolution slider" element. Drop this into the page.

There's a settings cog in the element you dropped into the page. Click this, you'll open the settings window, choose the revolution slider you need. That's it!

Full width slider in a page

First, make sure your publsihed page is set to the "fullwidth" page template.

After following the first steps above, you'll still have a boxed slider, now what you need to do is open your Row settings (cog on orange background) and change the display mode (first select item on the left) from "boxed" to "full" (or similar.) Next, scroll along the settings until you get to the vertical padding settings. Reduce this to 0 and then publish your chanes. You now have a full width revolution slider.
