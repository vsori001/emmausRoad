Theme Support

Due to an influx of customisation requests, please ensure that your ticket relates to theme usage, setup, bugs or feature requests. Customisation (theme modification, behaviour changes, moving elements etc.) requests will be politely forwarded to -- http://www.madeinebor.com/customisations/ -- and then this ticket will be closed.

I run support Weekdays 9am-5pm GMT. You can expect a response within 24 - 48 hours Monday to Friday unless I have other appointments / national holiday which will delay my answer. I'll respond to questions outside of these times & weekends occasionally, but please don't expect a response outside my stated hours.

What my tickets are for:
Help using themes, Help setting up themes, Bug reports & fixes, Feature requests

What my tickets aren't for:
Help with, or integration of, 3rd Party Plugins, Customisation of my themes, 'My client needs this'.

What counts as customisation?
If you want to change the appearance or functionality of the theme by editing or extending the theme's code, then that is customisation, and will not be supported through my tickets.

Do you need customisation work?
If that's exactly what you're here for, we've partnered with a leading theme customisation company, you can fill out a custom work form here; http://www.madeinebor.com/customisations/

Things to check when leaving a ticket:

  1. Validate your purchase with the ticket, no help will be offered unless the purchase code validates
  2. For EVERY ticket, please leave a link to your site, this will help answer your question more quickly
  3. If you have an issue that you can take a screenshot of & post a link to your site, then even better
  4. Remember to disable all Plugins/Extensions/Non-Standard Widgets first, you'd be surprised how many times this will fix your issue :)
  5. Lastly, please remember I do offer full support for free, which is not a requirement of selling on Themeforest, please be polite, and leave your tickets as clear and concise as possible, don't post every single question that comes to your mind, be sure to investigate it first and ask me second, why? Well the less time spent on support, the more time I have to work on updates and add extra features to your theme to make it even more awesome.
