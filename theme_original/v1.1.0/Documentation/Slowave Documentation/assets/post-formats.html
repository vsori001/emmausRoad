Using Post Formats

Once you've got started using your various post types, you'll be interested in post formats, here's a quick overview of them

Video Post Format
The video post format will give you inputs underneath the WP Post editor for you to paste in your video links, check the following for details of the video links you can use:

Available video links

Audio Post Format
The audio post format gives you additional inputs underneath the WP Editor just like the video editor, you should add SoundCloud iFrames in here instead of an embed link however, the theme will not do the work for you with this post format.

Image Post Format
The Image Post Format gives you an "upload images" button underneath the WP Post Editor, upload images to here, they will then be displayed in the post accordingly.

Gallery Post Format
The gallery Post Format gives you an "upload images" button underneath the WP Post Editor, upload images to here, they will then be displayed in the post accordingly. You can switch posts between the image and gallery post format as you wish since they share this control :)

All other post formats are purely for your own organisational requirements, for the audio post format, you can place soundcloud URLs directly into the Post Editor, and the theme will turn these into soundcloud players, as long as the URL is on it's own line.
