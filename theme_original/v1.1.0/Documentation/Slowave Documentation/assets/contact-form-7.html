Contact Form 7

We use the Contact Form 7 plugin in slowave, as it's simple & easy to customise to your needs.

Full contact form 7 details can be found here:

Contact Form 7

Contact form 7 should be installed by default by the Slowave plugins importer when you first install the theme, if not you can grab it from the "plugins" section of your WP Dashboard.

Contact form 7 lets you build your layout with shortcodes, see below for what we use in the Slowave demo:

[text* your-name placeholder "Name (Required)"][email* your-email placeholder "Email (Required)"][text* your-subject placeholder "subject"][textarea your-message][submit "Submit"]
